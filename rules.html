WTF ESPORTS TOURNAMENT RULES

🎮 Game Rules

  • Players must join the room before the match start time.
  • Teaming with other teams is strictly prohibited.
  • Use of hacks, mods, or illegal software will result in a permanent ban.
  • Players must use their registered game ID.
  • Players must follow fair play guidelines.

📋 Booking Rules

  • Entry fee must be paid before registration confirmation.
  • Payment screenshot must be uploaded correctly.
  • Only one team per mobile number is allowed.
  • Registration closes when slots are full.
  • Admin decision is final regarding slot confirmation.

🏆 Tournament Rules

  • Room ID and Password will be shared before the match.
  • Players must join within the given time.
  • Late joining may lead to disqualification.
  • Placement points and kills will determine the final score.

⚠ Player Behaviour Rules

  • No abusive language in game chat.
  • Respect other players and admins.
  • Toxic behaviour may lead to disqualification.

💰 Refund Policy

  • Entry fees are non-refundable.
  • If the match is cancelled by admin, refund or reschedule will be provided.

🚫 Disqualification Rules

  • Use of hacks or cheats.
  • Fake payment screenshot.
  • Teaming with other squads.
  • Using unregistered player.
